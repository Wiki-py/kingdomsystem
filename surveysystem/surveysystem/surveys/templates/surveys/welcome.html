OBB Research Team (OBBRT) Initiative

Cultural Transformation Initiative
Background & Context

Cultural transformation is rapidly re-shaping our global economy, permeating every clan and aspects of daily life – changing the way we learn, work, trade, socialize, and access public and private services and information.

OBB Research Team (OBBRT) Mission

In recognition of this trend, The OBB KINGDOM had to create an independent segment of OBB Research Team (OBBRT) with the objective of reaching over Twenty Five (25) clans, and 260,000 estimated population underserved in OBB communities.

For the last one year, The Team have consulted with the Kingdom Executive Committees to ensure it comes out with comprehensive baseline/Survey tool to enable it reach over and answer all Clan/sub-clans/Ridge questions.

Program Benefits

The Program provides a catalytic blend of data collection tools, skilling, and networks to bring opportunities in the OBB closer to young people, women, and small businesses in hard-to-reach OBB communities.

Together, we are leveraging shared Cultural infrastructure and partnerships to enable increased access to essential services, products, market, funding, and employment in this burgeoning OBB and Uganda at large.

Survey Objectives
  • Reach over 25 clans and 260,000 population
  • Comprehensive baseline data collection
  • Answer Clan/Sub-clan/Ridge specific questions
  • Enable data-driven decision making
  • Support cultural transformation initiatives
